About Me

I was born on Oahu, Hawaii, but for most of my life, I've resided in sunny San Diego. I'm the first-born of three rambunctious children and a proud member of a mixed-race family.

My mom's family comes from Saint Vincent, an island in the Caribbean. I visited once as a baby, but I haven't been back since. I'm hoping to return someday.

We're not as sure about my dad's heritage but he has northwestern European ancestry.


I was introduced to computer programming during high school, at an IDTech Java summer camp. After that, I wanted to learn more, so I took a Computer Science class at my school. Taking that class gave me the push to pursue this field as a career, and I have yet to look back.

Since I was pretty young I've been into video games. Even before my brother started playing with the controller plugged in, the two of us have bonded over our shared interest. Unfortunately for him, I was always just a teeny bit better ;)

I started with mainly racing games, then I graduated to Call of Duty and Battlefield. I had a brief stint with RPG's, and my latest love has been Soulslike games. At the moment I find myself occupied with Sekiro and Dark Souls 2.


In part due to my early involvement with Gran Turismo, I've always found cars really neat. For most of my life my knowledge was limited to trivia, but a year ago I got my first car to work on and learn the mechanical side of things.

In addition to the above, I recently (2 years ago) got into fitness. Once high school ended and I no longer participated in organized sports, I wanted something to do to stay active. My dad had some dumbells in the garage and I gave them a try. I didn't enjoy it at first but over time it's become a sort of therapy for me.
